About the bakery

In 1863, five years after immigrating to the United States from Torreta, a small town outside Palermo Italy, Pieril Pierilini brought the art of Italian pastry making to the internet, now this is what you get, Pieril Pierilini on the interwet. Finally you can order pie from your bedroom.

Pierils history

Pierils’s motto is: “If its not good enough for me, its not good enough for my customers” and therefore prides himself on using only the highest quality ingredients. Pieril's products are all made from scratch on premises and have no preservative and no trans fat.

Pierilini Pieril

There is only one Pierilini Pieril.
At the age of 18, with no formal training, he started out washing dishes at his own bakery and began learning the art of baking from authentic old world pastry chefs and bakers from Italy. Pieril started the pie company from nothing and is still to this day our director of taste.
My name is Pieril - Pieril
